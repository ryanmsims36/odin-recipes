Cereal

Description:

Nothing beats the basics! Optimize your flavor right at the store! Good for Saturday mornings and for a quick meal!

Ingredients:

  • Cereal (your choice!)
  • Milk (Again, your choice!)

Steps:

  1. First you will need a bowl and a spoon!
  2. Next, pour the ceral of your choice into the bowl. Make sure to leave room for milk!
  3. Now pour the milk into the bowl of cereal, adjusting the ratio of milk to cereal as you like. Be sure to add milk after cereal, we are not monsters.
  4. Now put on a good cartoon and enjoy your cereal!
