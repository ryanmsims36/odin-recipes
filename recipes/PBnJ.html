Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description:

A delectable Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich. As delicious as it is easy to make. The best bet for struggling college students.

Ingredients:

  • Bread (Preferably Brioche)
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps:

  1. Take two slices of bread and lay them on a plate
  2. Using a butter knife, get some peanut butter and spread it one slice of the bread.
  3. After cleaning off your knife to prevent cross contamination, spread Jelly using the knife on the other slice of bread.
  4. Now press both slices together and enjoy!
