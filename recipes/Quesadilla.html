Quesadilla

Description:

Nothing beats the simplicity of cheese and tortilla. This dish is also a great way to sample different salsas. Yumm!

Ingredients:

  • Flour Tortillas
  • Shredded Mexican Cheese
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Take two Tortillas and place them on a plate.
  2. Generously sprinkle the cheese on one of the tortillas.
  3. Rest the other tortilla on top of the cheese.
  4. Add some olive oil to a cast iron and set to medium heat.
  5. Once the cast iron is hot, add the tortillas with cheese to the pan and allow to cook for 5 minutes
  6. Once the cheese has melted, remove the quesadilla from the pan and serve on a plate with salsa and enjoy!
